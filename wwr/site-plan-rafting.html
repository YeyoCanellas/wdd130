Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide exciting and unforgettable outdoor adventures for adrenaline seekers and nature enthusiasts alike. With a focus on safety, professionalism and environmental management. We want you to have an exciting experience sailing through wild rivers and impressive landscapes. On this website, we want to inspire a love of nature and foster a community of adventure seekers who share our passion for outdoor exploration and sustainable recreation

Audience

Our target audience is made up of adventurous people between 18 and 50 years old, both thrill-seekers and outdoor activity enthusiasts. These people often lead active lifestyles and seek experiences that offer excitement and connection to nature. They are generally financially stable and have disposable income for recreational activities. By accessing our website, they expect to find detailed information about rafting trips, including difficulty levels, at what age they can participate in the activities, safety measures and the equipment provided. Additionally, they seek guidance on travel planning. Given their active lifestyles, our audience primarily accesses our website via mobile devices while on the go, allowing for convenient trip booking and updates.
